Learn about my honours project and my research within nanovaccines.

Formulating Chitosan-based Nanocapsules and Nanoparticles for delivery of ovalbumin

Lipid based nanoparticles for chitosan delivery

I conducted this research to improve two previous formulations obtained from previous research papers Cordiero et al (2019) and Pergo et al (2010). Both research papers were investigating the effects of particle size on the bioavailability of the drug. They both found that there is a direct correlataion between size of the particles and bioavailability, smaller particle size means higher bioavailability. Cordiero et al (2019) tested this by delivering nanocapsules into the lymph nodes of tumor infected mice and measuring the bioavailability of the drug around the tumor. In my research, I formulated two chitosan based formulations for nanocapsules and nanospheres to acheive lower average particle size than that acheived in these papers.
