Wordle!

My biggest project on python has been a wordle clone with a little twist made using pygame. The clone gives you the word in Arabic as a clue and you'll have to spell it in English. This is aimed at Arabic speakers who are wanting to expand their english vocabulary and improve spelling. One of the biggest hurdles during this project was matching the english word-list to the arabic word-list alongside translating the english list into arabic. I believe this task can be automated so I will be learning more about automation on python to achieve this.
