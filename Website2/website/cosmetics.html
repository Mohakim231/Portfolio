FMCG

Other the than knowledge gained through my bachelors degree, I became really intrested in fast moving consumer goods. Most in particular those used in personal care as these products surround us around the house. Here is some of those that I took a particular intrest in:

Liquid Soap

I've formulated a liquid hand wash during the first lockdown, this was an intresting process as it was my very first expirence researching for a product without guidence.

Ingrediants used:

SLES, Cocamidopropyl betaine, Sodium chloride, Sodium benzoate, Citric acid, Glycerin, Aqua ,Colouring.

Toothpaste

After my first project was complete I started learning more about FMCG cosmetics and also their manufacturing processes. I started reading articles and then books on toothpaste manufacturing as well as the machienery used to make and package it. Some ingrediants that are essential in toothpaste making were not obtained as they were toxic (sodium floride).

Ingrediants used:

SLS, Hydrated silica, Glycerin, Sorbitol, Titanium dioxide, Aqua.

Cosmetic formulations at DMU:

As part of my bachelors in science I took cosmetic science where we formulated and made several products in the lab. I chose my favourites from my lab book to share on here:

Foundation Cream

A cream to aid the adhesion of powder or makeup to the skin.

Ingrediants used:

Isopropyl myristate, stearic acid, Cetostearyl alcohol, Potassium hydroxide, Glycerol, Phenoxyethanol, Purified water, Fragrance.

Mascara

Ingrediants used:

Isopropyl myristate, stearic acid, Carnauba wax, White beeswax, Abracol SLG, Propyl Butex, Triethanolamine, Sodium alginate, Titanium Dioxide, Blue 1142, Black 1430.

Lipstick

Ingrediants used:

Carnauba wax, Ozokerite wax, Hydrous wool fat, Castor oil, Titanium Dioxide, Colour.
